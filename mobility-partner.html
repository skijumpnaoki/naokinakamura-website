Who you're partnering with

Naoki Nakamura is a Japanese professional ski jumper competing on the FIS World Cup circuit. The van is Naoki Nakamura's activity vehicle — the one that actually carries him and his equipment across Europe all winter.

The sustainability part

Every kilometre the van drives is logged and its CO₂ offset by purchasing and retiring JCX carbon credits. You receive a carbon-offset certificate issued in your company's name — evidence you can attach to ESG or IR reporting.
